Stardew Valley

By Monica Garcia
Published on December 9th, 2021.

Stardew Valley is an indie game developed and published by ConcernedApe which released on February 26, 2016. You have just inherited your grandfather's delapidated farm in the town of Stardew Valley and it is up to you to bring it back to life. The town of Stardew Valley has a lot to offer the player besides farming - you can chat with the other villagers, jump into the dangerous mines and bring back treasures and materials, fish in the bodies of water, and many other fun activities.

Find it on:

Pros Cons
Uniquely charming - the visuals are strikingly beautiful and the villagers are fun to get to know Not for those who do not like to grind - you will spend many hours trying to accomplish goals
Lots to do for $15 - you are not limited to just farming! You have mining, fishing, foraging, making friends, and so on! As well as many secret objectives that you can stumble upon No opening tutorial - this game is very trial and error, you learn as you go which is not for everyone
Great soundtrack - very calming and peaceful
Final Score: 9/10

Fallout New Vegas

By Monica Garcia
Published on November 22th, 2021.

Fallout New Vegas is a post-apocalyptic RPG developed by Obsidian Entertainment and published by Bethesda Softworks. It was released in the U.S. on October 19th, 2010. You are a Courier who is in charge of delivering a platinum chip to the mysterious Mr.House, the owner of the New Vegas Strip. However, trouble finds you on your way as you are ambushed by a group of unknown men. You are shot and left for dead, but a doctor from Good Springs finds you and nurses you back to health. You do not remember anything about your past so you are left to figure out what you stand for in the Mojave Desert. There are plenty of side quests to do, characters to meet, and gangs to get in your way or who you can team up with. The choice is yours!

Find it on:

Pros Cons
The side quests are fun and engaging - you get to meet interesting characters and learn a lot about the Mojave Desert and its people Known for having a lot of bugs - especially with loading and saving your game which is frustrating
The radio is one of the best parts - lots of tunes that are perfectly themed with the setting The weapon and ammo creating features could be better - I found myself avoiding this feature almost completely as it was largely unnecessary and seemed more trouble than it was worth
Tons of cool and interestingly themed locations on the map to explore - you never know what you are going to run into!
Final Score: 9.5/10
